SCHOOL PUPIL LEADER ELECTION ACADEMIC YEAR 2022-2023





As you know, we are in the midst of our school election. This is an important opportunity for us to learn about democracy and the importance of civic engagement. It is also an opportunity for us to develop important skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication. When we elect candidates to represent us, we are essentially giving them our power to make decisions on our behalf. That is why it is so important to choose candidates who are qualified and who share our values. I urge you to take this election seriously. Do your research on the candidates and vote for the one who you believe will best represent you and your interests. Candidate election in school is not just about choosing who will represent us. It is also about learning about the democratic process and developing the skills we need to be active citizens.

-Principal
